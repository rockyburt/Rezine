Community

The most important thing for a successful Open Source project is a good and vibrant community. You can help TextPress not only by writing plugins or hacking on the core code, but also by just blogging about it and letting people know that it exists.

If you are blogging about TextPress, send us a mail and we add you to the planet which aggregates blogs by the TextPress developers and TextPress users that want to share their experiences.

If you want to talk with the TextPress developers or some TextPress users? our IRC Channel with your favourite IRC client and chat about the future of TextPress, ask questions and get help.
