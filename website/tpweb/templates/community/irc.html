IRC Channel

TextPress is one of the pocoo project and uses the #pocoo channel on irc.freenode.net for both development specific questions and user support.

You need an IRC Client to join it. The following clients are widely known:

  • Pidgin, Windows, Linux, OS X
  • X-Chat, Linux
  • mirc, Windows
  • colloquy, OS X

We also have a bot in the channel that logs the conversations. You can have a look at older discussions in the logs.
